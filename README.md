## What is xBP?

xBP is a clean, private health tracker built especially for elderly users and their families.  
Everything stays on your phone — no internet, no accounts, no data sold.  
Just install, log your readings, and export photos when you need to share them.

Perfect for:
- Tracking hypertension
- Caring for aging parents/grandparents
- Doctors or caregivers who want simple, private records
- Anyone who just wants to keep their health data safe and simple

---

## Key Features

### For Seniors & Caregivers
- Super simple logging (just 3 fields: Systolic, Diastolic, Pulse)
- Clear status messages (“High blood pressure – Stage 1”)
- Dark, calm interface that’s easy on the eyes
- One-tap export as a photo (double-tap on preview to save)
- Voice input coming soon (speak your reading instead of typing)

### For Doctors & Caregivers
- Export individual readings as photos
- Export full week or all readings as a clean PDF
- Medical summary report (“3 readings this week – average systolic 126”)
- Calendar export option
- Keep all data completely private and local

### Core Benefits
- 100% local storage — zero risk of data loss
- No subscriptions, no accounts, no tracking
- Works offline forever
- Free basic version available
- Paid version ($9.99 one-time) unlocks voice input, PDF exports, and more
